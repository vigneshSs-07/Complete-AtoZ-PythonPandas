#LogLoss
https://gombru.github.io/2018/05/23/cross_entropy_loss/
Log loss can be extended for multiple classes.

what we have covered is called a binary cross-entropy (in broader sense), where we have only two classes. Usually the classification problem is as follows.

Whether an observation is desired class or not. {0,1}

"negative = 0

neutral = 1

positive = 2"

In case of multiclass classification:

Instead of asking: Whether observation is class {1,2,3}

I can decompose the question as follows:

1. Whether my observation is class 1 or not

2. Whether my observation is class 2 or not

3. Whether my observation is class 3 or not

and sum up their individual log loss ( categorical cross entropy)

Note: I am not including the math. For mathematical explanation, refer to 

https://gombru.github.io/2018/05/23/cross_entropy_loss/
